Client Project Scope

Delivering tailored and sustainable business solutions for a UK-based network provider.

Client Project Introduction & Client Needs Analysis

The client is a leading UK-based network provider specializing in high-speed internet, mobile communication services, and cloud-based solutions. The company faces challenges related to aging IT infrastructure, underutilized data analytics, ineffective marketing strategies, and the need for sustainable operations.

  • Challenges: Outdated systems, lack of data insights, ineffective customer engagement, and sustainability concerns.
  • Key Business Drivers: Operational efficiency, customer retention, and sustainability.
  • Stakeholders: Internal teams, customers, regulatory bodies, and industry partners.

Client Project Scope Definition & Ethics Protocol Requirements

The project will focus on IT modernization, data analytics integration, targeted marketing campaigns, and sustainable initiatives. Exclusions include hardware upgrades and customer-facing app development due to scope constraints.

  • Ethics Protocol: Compliance with GDPR standards, inclusivity, and transparency throughout the project lifecycle.

Client Project Plan & Risk Register

Project Plan

  1. Phase 1: Assessment and planning (2 weeks).
  2. Phase 2: Strategy development (4 weeks).
  3. Phase 3: Implementation and monitoring (6 weeks).

Risk Register

Risk Likelihood Impact Mitigation Strategy
Data Breaches Medium High Implement encryption and conduct security audits.
Resistance to Change High Medium Conduct stakeholder training sessions.
System Downtime Medium High Schedule migrations during off-peak hours.

Client Project Success Criteria

  • 30% reduction in IT downtime within three months.
  • 25% increase in customer retention through loyalty programs.
  • 15% reduction in energy usage within the first year.
  • Improved marketing campaign ROI by 20% within six months.

Client Project Reflection

This project demonstrates the value of aligning consultancy practices with sustainability and ethical principles. By focusing on operational efficiency and customer engagement, it highlights the long-term benefits of data-driven insights and stakeholder inclusivity.
